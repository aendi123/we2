Box Sizing Fixed Height + Overflow

Fixed sizes of heights with overflowing text and overflow one of visible | hidden | scroll

  • .box is lightgray; sized as border-box 200px heigh; padding t+b 10px, border t+b coral 50px; margin t+b 25px
  • .container has padding t+b 10px and border t+b lightcyan 20px
  • Height of container elements, if not explicitly specified is sized to fit the size of its subelements
  • Text can flow out of the content box when nothing is specified ("overflow: visible" is the default)
  • With overflow: hidden or scroll overflowing text does not extend beyond the inner border of box
